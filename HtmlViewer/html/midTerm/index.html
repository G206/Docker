Ride that is considered the best to look forward to - Tatsu at Magic Mountain

Upon opening, Tatsu was the tallest, fastest, and longest flying roller coaster in the world. Suspended beneath a steel track, riders experience a total of 263 feet (80 m) in elevation changes while harnessed in a prone position. It also features the worlds largest pretzel loop.
